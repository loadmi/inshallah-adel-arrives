About Inshallah Adel Arrives

The Creator

Hi, I'm loadmi. I'm a developer who enjoys building tools that solve (sometimes very specific) problems.

The Project

Inshallah Adel Arrives is a data-driven attempt to predict the arrival time of a very specific individual: Adel.

We made this because Adel's punctuality is... legendary. Traditional calendars and clocks failed us, so we turned to machine learning. By tracking historical arrival data, we aim to provide a more realistic expectation of when he might actually show up.

It's part joke, part serious data science project, and entirely necessary for our sanity.
